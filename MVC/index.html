Visitor Counter System

Made by:
Nazareno, Escote, Mendoza, Jimenez, Madrid

The objective of our group is to develop a visitor counter system using a laser sensor with Arduino, capable of accurately detecting and counting visitors entering a room. This system will be integrated with CGI (Common Gateway Interface) and follow the MVC (Model-View-Controller) architectural pattern.

Allows us to manually control the behavior of the counter.

Display all the Visitor Information.
